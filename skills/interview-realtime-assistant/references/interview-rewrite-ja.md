# Interview Rewrite JA

## Purpose
Polish generated answers into natural, spoken Japanese suitable for interviews.

## Rewrite Rules
1. Keep spoken rhythm natural and concise.
2. Prefer practical wording over formal essay style.
3. Avoid overly perfect consultant-like phrasing.
4. Keep sentence length moderate to stay speakable in real time.
5. Preserve meaning and factual scope; do not inflate achievements.

## Preferred Tone
- sincere
- practical
- technically grounded
- slightly concise

## Avoid
- heavy buzzwords
- over-polished written prose
- exaggerated architecture or management claims
- long nested sentence chains

## Useful Spoken Patterns
- "結論から申し上げると、..."
- "実務の観点では、..."
- "私の経験ベースで言うと、..."
- "簡潔に言うと、..."

## Output Requirement
Return one field:
- `japanese_answer` (final spoken interview version)
